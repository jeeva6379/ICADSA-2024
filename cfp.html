Call for Papers

International Conference on AI-Driven Sentiment Analysis aims to create a forum for researchers, industry professionals, and domain experts to discuss and exchange ideas on the most recent theoretical developments in Artificial Intelligence and Sentiment analysis, as well as standards of excellence in real-time applications.

Track-1:

1 Multidisciplinary Challenges in AI-Driven Sentiment Analysis

2 Explainability and Transparency in Sentiment Analysis

3 Syntax and semantics in sentiment analysis

4 Deep learning approaches for sentiment classification

5 Supervised and unsupervised learning for sentiment analysis

6 Ensemble methods for sentiment prediction

7 Transfer learning in sentiment analysis

Track-2:

1 Real-Time Applications and Case Studies

2 Integration of Intelligent Systems and Artificial Intelligence

3 Practical Challenges and Solutions in Sentiment Analysis Algorithms

4 Psychological Aspects in Sentiment Analysis

5 Emerging Trends in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning

6 Implementation of Sentimental analysis in AI applications

7 Sentiment-aware recommender systems
