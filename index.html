About the Conference

Sentiment analysis, a fundamental natural language processing (NLP) activity, plays a crucial role in text processing systems. It is widely employed for the analysis and comprehension of texts, holding significance for both the scientific and business communities. As an integral component of artificial intelligence (AI), sentiment analysis encounters challenges that necessitate broad multidisciplinary input and a focus on explainability.

This conference addresses and delves into the challenges of AI-based analysis in real-time scenarios. The International Conference on AI-Driven Sentiment Analysis will spotlight intelligent systems and artificial intelligence, exploring their applications in the real world. The conference will emphasize the practical challenges encountered and the solutions implemented in the domains of Sentiment Analysis Algorithms and Applications.

Sentiment analysis is an interesting emerging field of study within the Artificial Intelligence integrating Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Psychology.
