Submission Guidelines:

Authors are encouraged to submit original research papers, case studies, and innovative applications in the field of AI-driven sentiment analysis. Submissions will undergo a rigorous peer-review process, and accepted papers will be presented at ICADSA 2024.

Send a full manuscript to this email address: icadsaconference@gmail.com

Submitted papers will undergo a rigorous peer-review process.

✔ Submissions must present original work that has not been previously published or is not currently under review elsewhere.

✔ All submissions and presentations should be in English.

✔ Accepted papers will be presented orally during the conference.

✔ Authors are required to initiate the conference registration process promptly upon receiving the official acceptance letter.

✔ At least one author for each accepted paper must register for the conference to ensure its inclusion in the proceedings.

Plagiarism Policy

 

∘ Submitted papers must have a plagiarism level of less than 15% and single source less than 5%,

∘ Proper citation of references is mandatory. Authors should ensure accurate referencing following the specified citation style.

∘ Authors are expected to adhere to the highest standards of ethical conduct in research.

∘  Any form of ethical misconduct, including but not limited to data fabrication, plagiarism, and simultaneous submission to multiple conferences, is strictly prohibited.

∘ The conference committee will conduct thorough checks for plagiarism and ethical misconduct.

∘ Papers not meeting the specified criteria may be subject to rejection or exclusion from the proceedings.
